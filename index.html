ABOUT ME

I'm a third year Computer Engineering student @ the University of Waterloo. I am versatile, high-spirited, vivacious individual and definitely a rational optimist (subtle plug to the book The Rational Optimist which I'm reading rn!). I am an incredibly curious person and love learning about practically anything, and am constantly on the pursuit to stumble upon things that gauge my interests. This is one reason why my 2018 goal was to read at least 10 books.

Software-wise I am the most intrigued by the Payments world (I'm currently interning at Shopify on the Payments team - I will be writing a blog post about that soon!), data analytics and data infrastructure (such as ETL pipelines, databases, etc.) and more recently, compilers and operating systems. As you can probably tell, these are all vastly different - which comes to the point that I consider myself a generalist. (TODO: write a blog post about this too). As someone who is just at the beginning on their career and as someone who has just gotten into software development recently, I'm am trying to spend the few upcoming years (still in school + doing internships) acting like a sponge and just absorbing as much about everything as possible.
